Laboratory

In Laboratory, it is the campus of Kleinburg University itself, which narrates the story. For centuries, this neurotic institution has secretly employed objects, animals, and selected staff members to maintain its preferred version of campus life. Growing infatuated with a particular student, Kasimir Schneider, the campus enables the young man's obsession with parapsychology and the afterlife—with dire consequences. Watching Kasimir's corpse being carried away, the campus addresses its favorite student one last time, reminiscing about their time together and wondering where things went wrong.
