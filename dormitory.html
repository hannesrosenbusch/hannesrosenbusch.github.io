After causing a fatal zoo accident, a girl overdoses in her mother's attic. On a far-away campus, a lonely cafeteria employee is killed in the name of science. Meeting in the afterlife, the unlikely duo is welcomed by a silly angel-boy who pushes people towards allegedly life-changing insights, hoping to wake them from their near-death experiences. Unfortunately, the angel has a very poor understanding of human psychology, solely relying on academic publications to design his reanimation program. As their brains shut down, our heros experience ever-stranger things, and a 'life worth living' seems out of reach.
