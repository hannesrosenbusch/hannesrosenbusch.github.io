Campus

This story is told by the spirit of Kleinburg University. For centuries, it has inhabited the statues, gardens, and buildings on campus, silently manipulating its staff and students. When a young man named Kasimir Schneider enrolls in the parapsychology program, the campus spirit becomes enamoured, constantly shadowing its new protégé. Kasimir, sensing the eerie presence accompanying his every move, gets lost in the world of paranormal research-with dire consequences. His lifeless body is soon found in the underground laboratories and as he gets carried off-campus, the spirit hovers above him, feverishly addressing its beloved student... wondering where things went wrong.
